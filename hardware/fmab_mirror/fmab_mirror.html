FMAB Mirror

Custom laser-cut acrylic mirror inspired by Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Project Summary

Why buy decor when you can build something that actually means something to you? This mirror started as a screenshot from Fullmetal Alchemist and ended up laser-cut in acrylic on my wall. First try. Learned a lot.

  • Custom-designed acrylic mirror inspired by "The Gate" from Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood
  • Designed in Adobe Illustrator, laser-cut and engraved on 32" x 20" x 1/8" acrylic
  • Engraved lines filled with black Sharpie for contrast
  • Functional, meaningful room decor
